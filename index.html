Shaping Tomorrow's Innovators

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education that develops students academically and practically, building capable and responsible students for the future.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's leading tech-integrated school.

Hi Tech Academy was founded in 1999 in the historic city of Harar, Ethiopia by Mr. Helina Haile. What began as a small computer training center has grown into a full K-12 institution with 7 branches across Harar city, serving over 3200 students with 150 dedicated staff members. Located at Shash Rd, Harar.
